Provide as much detail of their experience as you can. Do not include their name or the names of other potential victims (if any).

What information to include

A detailed report should have information that includes:

  • how the potential victim was treated
  • what an average day was like
  • if they could not leave, why not
  • what their living conditions were like

If they were trafficked, it helps to know:

  • where they were recruited
  • details of the journeys they took
  • who, if anyone, travelled with them
  • who arranged the journey
